About Me

Hello!

My name is David McGowan, a creative web developer and designer from Glasgow. I am extremely enthusiastic about front-end web design in which I hope to continually advance my skillsets.

I started off managing e-commerce websites for previous employers over the course of 8 years. In 2011 I decided that I wanted to learn more about the development aspect and swapped my employment for a place at the City of Glasgow College studying Interactive Media. I then progressed on to studying a Web and Mobile Development BSc at the University of the West of Scotland. I am now undertaking my final years honours degree, which I am expected to complete in May 2016.

I believe that the skills and knowledge I have gained through my studies along with the experience of once being a client has enabled me to better understand my own client’s requirements.
