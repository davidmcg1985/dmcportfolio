About Me.

My name is David McGowan, a web developer from Glasgow currently working for Screenmedia. I am extremely enthusiastic about all things web!

I began managing e-commerce websites for previous employers over the course of 8 years. In 2011 I decided that I wanted to learn more about the development aspect and swapped my employment for a place at the City of Glasgow College studying Interactive Media. I then progressed on to study Web and Mobile Development BSc (Hons) at the University of the West of Scotland where I achieved a first class honours award.

My work experience combined with the skills I have gained at university have helped me understand projects from both a client and developers perspective. I am focused on expanding my skillset and strive to learn new languages and technologies in my own personal time
